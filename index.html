Forestry Alumni Association
Welcome to the website of FAAO.

As a compelling organization, keen on the excellence of College Of Forestry, OUAT, Bhubaneswar and its Alumni, the Alumni Association strives to provender a lifelong intellectual and emotional relationship with the college and amongst Alumni; and to provide sustainable platform for effective networking, support ,development .The head quarter of the Association is located in the premises of the College Of Forestry under Orissa University of Agriculture and Technology (OUAT), Bhubaneswar, Pin-751003.

Objectives of FAAO

  1. To bring about an effective, strong and mutually beneficial networking between the members of the alumni and to nutriment the culture of philanthropy for the development of society.

  2. To establish closer interaction between related national and international bodies having similar objectives.

  3. To conduct seminars, conferences, workshops and meetings of Forestry and allied professionals and faculties for the purpose of promotion of Forestry education, research, extension and awareness among the people and to participate in any other worthwhile national and International development activities.

  4. To recognize and support scholarship and academic excellence of the college.

Alumni Activities

  1. Annual reunions

    Since its inception for its members providing platform for interaction and socializing. The Association also intends to attract individuals of repute or useful experience in forestry sector or environmental and allied fields to join the community.

  2. Social work

    Organising, sponsoring or carrying out collaborative projects, e.g. Plantation programmes, Environment related campaigns like public awareness programmes, forestry, environmental and socio economic surveys, philanthropic activities like Blood donation camps, etc.

  3. Technical and consultant roles

    Core forestry and allied institutions and organizations of government or private links are welcome to derive technical and consultancy benefits from the alumni members.

  4. Career Counseling and placement assistance to B.Sc. (Forestry) and M.Sc. (Forestry) students

    Unceasing efforts are being invested to improve the placement quantum of students on a broader base of sectors. Career counseling of senior students is carried out for proper guidance in making choices of the line of work.

  5. Organizing seminars and workshops

    Several seminars and workshops at the state and national level are in pipeline regarding environment, forestry, socio economic issues of national and global importance.
