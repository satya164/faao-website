Members

There are five categories of Members viz.,

  1. Donors

    Those who pay or donate a lump sum of not less than Rs.10,000/-.

  2. Patron Members

    Those who pay or donate a lump sum of not less than Rs.5000/-.

  3. Life Members

    Those who pay a one-time subscription of Rs.2,000/-, past and present faculty members of OUAT, Bhubaneswar.

  4. Annual Members

    Those who pay a lump sum of Rs 500/-.

  5. Associate Members

    Those who are not old students with B. Sc. (Forestry) degree from OUAT are eligible to become Associate Donor Members or Associate Patron Members on payment of the subscription prescribed as above without voting right.
